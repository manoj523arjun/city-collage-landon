OUR VISION

CITY COLLEGE LONDON vision is to provide educational qualifications which ensures student's positive career progression through employment, higher education and entrepreneurship.

OUR AIM

The aim is to provide higher and further education to students for achieving desired qualifications, skills and entrepreneurial abilities for a positive career progression.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • Provide high quality higher and further education.
  • Add value to their current qualifications, skills and experiences.
  • Increase motivation and self esteem.
  • Enhance their employability and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Meeting the skills gap of the local community.
  • Continuous improvement of the services through monitoring and review.

PROFESSIONALS SHAPING THE FUTURE PROFESSIONAL

We are a group of Professionals shaping the future professionals. Our teachers are experts in their fields who have real enthusiasm for teaching. They support you at every step of your learning journey, with the great resource of their knowledge and wider experience in various sectors. Having teachers with industry experience enables applied learning.

SUPPORT

We offer a personalised learning experience with ample care and support to each student to ensure that you achieve your learning objectives. Student representatives are encouraged to participate in various academic and administrative committees of the College to share your opinions. Engaging with these management activities will help you prepare for future managerial roles. We provide continued Information, Advice and Guidance (IAG) through formal and informal channels for career progression, building soft skills and professional development, along with the curricular activities. Student enrichment through Entrepreneurs Club and Cultural club are some of the unique activities organised by the students and guided by a Club coordinator.
